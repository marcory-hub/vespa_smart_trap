**One-line purpose:** summary of BASE versions after beta test and feedback from acc
**Short summary:** end product is based on 3 versions BASE Lilygo T-SIM with custom PCB grove shield, -relay shield, -motor shield
**Agent:** here you find the links to the custom PCBs

---

- The BASE Lilygio T-SIM with **[Custom PCB Grove Shield](https://github.com/aggerritsen/T-SIMGroveShield) (final version),** enabling all plug and play components form the ONE. In that case you should accomodate the concerned Grove plug & play component as well like maybe some sensors..  
- The BASE Liltgo T-SIM with **[Custom PCB Relay Shield](https://github.com/aggerritsen/T-SIMRelayShield)** (final version), allowing you to use any I2C connected Grove component if and when required. In that case you could (if required) accommodate Grove plug & play component as well. Or you only use the 2 available relays  
- The The BASE Liltgo T-SIM with **[Custom PCB Motor Shield](https://github.com/aggerritsen/T-SIMMotorShield)** (still in test), for the use of 1 stepper motor or 2 DC motors, allowing you 2 GPIO contact for Grove relays, lights or other actuators. In that case you could (if required) accommodate only 2 Grove plug & play component as well, like maybe some sensors. Or you us only the available motors
